Who we are?

By connecting students all over the world to the best instructors, Chameleon Classes is helping individuals reach their goals and pursue their dreams. We are trying to build a space for comfortable learning experience.

Education is a human right. We are a nonprofit group because we believe in a free and widespread world-class education. Instead of ads or subscriptions, we are supported by individual contributions from people like you. Please join us and contribute in this noble task.

We’re a diverse team that has come together to work on an audacious mission: to provide a free world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Your little contribution can make a big difference.

Chameleon Classes is the trusted platform for education and learning. As a global nonprofit, Chameleon Classes is transforming traditional education, removing the barriers of cost, location and access. Fulfilling the demand for people to learn on their own terms, Chameleon Classes is reimagining the possibilities of education, providing the highest-quality, stackable learning experiences. Supporting learners at every stage, Chameleon Classes is where no one is left behind!
