Who we are?

By connecting students all over the world to the best instructors, Chameleon Classes is helping individuals reach their goals and pursue their dreams. We are trying to build a space for comfortable learning experience.

Education is a human right. We are a nonprofit group because we believe in a free and widespread world-class education. Instead of ads or subscriptions, we are supported by individual contributions from people like you. Please join us and contribute in this noble task.

Chameleon Classes is the trusted platform for education and learning. As a global nonprofit, Chameleon Classes is transforming traditional education, removing the barriers of cost, location and access. Fulfilling the demand for people to learn on their own terms, Chameleon Classes is reimagining the possibilities of education, providing the highest-quality, stackable learning experiences. Our services we offer

Learn Online
Online classes for flexible learning. Student can control their learning environment through deeper understanding.
Classroom Classes
For lots of ideas,discussion and for continuous interaction between student and teachers. We provide classroom learning and thinking beyond the classroom.
Home Tutions
To enhance the capacity and performance of the students. We help teachers and student to find each other to give you personalized course within your flexible schedule .
Personal Live Tutions
Online live tuitions for the students who want to interact personally with tutors.Face to face interaction and rapid feedback to all the problems would be effective for learning.
Educational Consultancy
Guidance,career choice, test preparation, college admission process, career counselling for students and boosting up confidence and help them to make decision about choosing right career.
Free NCERT Solutions
NCERT Book solutions of all the subjects will be provided for all the classes 6th to 12th in PDFs format. It will help them to delve deeper into the basics as well as advanced level of questions.
Free E-library
A digital library will be provided In PDFs format. Easily and rapidly clickable access to our resources. Explore our e-library to access the essentials resources available within.
